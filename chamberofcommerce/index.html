Welcome

Quakertown, Pennsylvania is a place like no other. From pastoral countryside and rolling hills, historical sites, family-owned farms and seasonal local produce to new investment by technology firms, manufacturing, distribution centers for major national brands and an eclectic mix of old and new family-owned businesses, Quakertown has much to offer those who visit, live, work and spend their time here.

Located within easy access points of the Pennsylvania Turnpike Northeast Extension, Quakertown is convenient to Philadelphia, the Lehigh Valley, the Poconos, New York City and New Jersey beaches. Growing business investment includes small and medium-sized firms, non-profit organizations, health care, restaurants, shopping.
