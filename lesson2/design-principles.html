White Space and Clean Design

LinkedIn

https://www.linkedin.com/

While I thought that LinkedIn's pages were too
busy, violating Occam's Razor, I feel their use of
White Space and Clean Design is very good. This is
evident in the spacing between content, buttons,
and sections. They made good use of color and
focused on information, rather than flashy graphics.

Visual Hierarchy

Temple University

https://www.temple.edu/

I feel that this site represents Visual Hierarchy
well. My eyes are drawn first to the article about
the new school president. Second, the bold color
of the COVID information banner drew my
attention. Finally, the Temple University logo
got its due. In addition to Visual Hierarchy, if
if you visit the site, I feel it is a good
representation of Repetition.

PARC: Repetition

State of Alaska

http://alaska.gov/

This site is a good example of repetition in the
styling of the side banners, the interesting use of
the mountain to tie the three links together in the
center, as well as the consistent styling of the
tabs. The calendar keeps the repetition going too.
